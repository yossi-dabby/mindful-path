# Adolescents CBT Core Series 1 - English

Approved English translation package for the adolescent CBT Core Series.

Audience: adolescents / teens ages 12-18  
Language: English  
Direction: LTR  
Total: 30 individual PDF worksheets  
Structure: 6 stages x 5 forms each

## Contents

- 30 individual PDF worksheets: `01-01-en.pdf` through `06-05-en.pdf`
- 6 stage-level full PDFs
- 1 combined full-series PDF: `adolescents-cbt-core-series-1-full-en.pdf`
- `manifest.adolescents-cbt-core-en.json`
- `QA_SUMMARY_EN.txt`
- `png-previews/` for visual review only

## AI catalog requirement

Use `manifest.adolescents-cbt-core-en.json` as the source of truth. The in-app AI should recommend forms by therapeutic content, intent, stage, worksheet number, and metadata - not by title alone.
